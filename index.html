Technologies used include HTML, and Tailwind for CSS, for front-end development, along with backend technologies for dynamic content management.

Load Locate (Lost and Found Website)

I created this website to complete the Web Programming and Testing course assignment. In this project we were divided into a group of 4 people. On this website, users can report items that they have found or lost along with the details of the item. On the website the user can also see all the lists of items that have been reported previously and also who reported. My job in this project is as a developer who is responsible for developing the website based on the design that has been made. Then I was also responsible for testing the functions on the website and testing the application programming interface (API).

Clustering Mall Customer Segmentation Data

I made this project to complete the Artificial Intelligence course assignment. In this project we were divided into a group of 4 people. My job in this project is as a programmer who is responsible for developing artificial intelligence models to classify customer data. In this project, we were assigned to classify customer data using the mean-shift method. From the classification results, it can be seen that there are 5 categories of customers, namely customers with low income and low spending score, customers with low income and high spending score, customers with high income and low spending score, customers with high income and high spending score and finally customers with middle income and middle spending score.
